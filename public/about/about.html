About Us

The Queer Film Club is a safe space for LGBTQ+ students in Denver to meet, learn about their history, and build relationships that foster a sense of community. This club considers all queer student applicants, so send us an email!


We're kicking the club off with The Birdcage, a Robin Williams comedy tackling the difficulties of being a closeted gay man in a homophobic world.
